Surface Logs from All Your Infrastructure
Reap the benefits of Terminal’s cloud logging and analytics without the overhead of migrating to a new infrastructure provider.  Use Terminal SDK to surface logs for every Ethereum JSON RPC endpoint you use in just a few lines of code. Additionally, logs are automatically surfaced for any Terminal enhanced RPC endpoints you use.

Surface Logs from MetaMask & other Wallet Providers 
Wrap injected web3 instances with Terminal SDK and aggregate logs across all the wallets your application utilizes in one place. View transactions and activity specific to your application and your users rather than looking at contracts on a block explorer. View human readable logs and analytics thanks to our automatic log parsing.

Surface Logs from Your Truffle Projects
Use Terminal SDK to initialize Terminal as a sub-provider in your Truffle development environment or use Terminal CLI to surface logs automatically for every new deployment, migration, and test. Easily track changes and debug throughout the development process and across your team and environments.
